Our Services

Flu Vaccination Provider Locations

Utilize the Google Maps API to discover a comprehensive list of flu vaccination providers in the U.S. This interactive map allows users to search by location or zip code, simplifying access to nearby service providers. Click on any map marker to reveal detailed information about each provider, and explore different regions efficiently.

Demographics for Flu Vaccination Coverage

View a detailed bar graph displaying flu vaccination rates among various demographic groups. Users can toggle between state and national data and select different reporting years. Customize your view by filtering the data by flu season, geography, age group, and race/ethnicity to gain better insights into vaccination trends.

Flu Season Vaccination Doses Distributed

Explore the distribution of flu vaccination doses through dynamic line and bar graphs that depict weekly cumulative data. This feature allows users to select specific weeks and flu seasons to filter the graph to their needs. The graph is accompanied by a dynamic description below, providing context and enhancing understanding of the trends shown.

Flu Hospitalization Rates

This visualization presents flu hospitalization rates per 100,000 individuals on a clear, informative line graph. Users can choose to view data in a weekly format or as cumulative rates over the course of a flu season. The filters allow for the selection of specific flu seasons, age, race/ethnicity, or other groupings to refine the data.
